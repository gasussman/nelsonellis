“Helping Chocolate Makers Make A Better Chocolate”

Making great chocolate is a process that begins the moment cacao pods are harvested from the tree. The time just after harvesting is when the bean gets its flavor. Too many variables change between one batch and the next, resulting in an uncertain quality from one harvest to the next.

We use scalable, cost-efficient technology to control these variables and ensure that every batch of cocoa is as good as the best batch.

By partnering with local farms we enable them to be more profitable by accessing premium markets.
